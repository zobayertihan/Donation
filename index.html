Our Donation Plan

We are planning so much to help people in this disaster. We are trying to provide shelter for them. We are trying to provide food for them. We are trying to offer them pure drinking water. We are trying to provide the medicine. We are trying to give them some educational activities.

Food for a family

To live, a family needs food and pure drinking water. So, we are trying to provide food and drinking water first. We are trying to give them a minimum amount of food and water for everyone.

Shelter for a family

After food, people need shelter to live. So we are trying to provide a hygienic place to live for them.

Education for a child

By this disaster, education processes are disturbed. That is harmful to a nation. We know "Education is the backbone of a nation". That's why we are trying to provide them with some educational activities.

We are dedicated to help them.

Natural disasters can have a life-altering impact on the individuals and families fortunate enough to survive them. But the effect of natural disasters can be felt at the community, city, and state level, or many times can impact an entire country. Natural disasters can have huge environmental impacts as well, even when human communities are relatively unaffected. How well the impact of a disaster event is absorbed has much to do with the intensity of the impact and the level of preparedness and resilience of the subject impacted. Natural disasters cause destruction of property, loss of financial resources, and personal injury or illness. The loss of resources, security, and access to the shelter can lead to massive population migrations in lesser-developed countries. By collecting donations we are helping people to fight these natural disasters. And we will do this till the end.
